# Dao Level Images — Additional Images For 18 Integral Levels

This file stores additional imagery and descriptions for the 18 Dao Resource Ladder levels.

Important:
These are supporting images, not a replacement for the 18-level model.
Do not convert the system into 8 steps with sublevels.

Main model:
18 integral levels.

---

## Level 1 — Storm / Odysseus

Additional images:
- Odysseus in a storm
- storm in the open sea
- person clinging to a broken piece of a boat

Meaning:
Critical instability. The person is in chaos and survival. Stabilization already feels like an almost impossible heroic task.

Task:
Find a point of support in chaos.

Result:
Internal state and health stabilize.

Final phrase:
"I can handle this. I can survive."
