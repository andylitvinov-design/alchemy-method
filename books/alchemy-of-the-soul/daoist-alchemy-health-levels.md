# Daoist Alchemy — Health, Success, Spirit Levels

Source: Alchemy of the Soul (Dao Magic) public materials

---

## Core Structure

The system defines 3 stages of inner alchemy:

1. Health / Body Resource (Lower Dantian)
2. Success / Social Realization (Middle Dantian)
3. Spirit / Dao Connection (Upper Dantian)

Each stage contains 7 levels.

Total: 18 levels.

---

## Resource Scale

The full range maps to resource growth from 1 to 10:

- 1–5 → health resource
- 5–8 → success resource (money, relationships)
- 9–10 → spiritual resource

---

## Core Cycle

1 Daoist Alchemy cycle:

- ≈ 1 level up
- ≈ 1 month
- ≈ 2 sessions

Typical starting point:

- around level 2
