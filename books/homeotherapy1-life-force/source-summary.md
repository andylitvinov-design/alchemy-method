The author describes a personal path:

- long-term interest in development technologies

- initial skepticism toward homeopathy

## 5. Four-Step Model Of Work

### Step 1 — Constellation / Situation Analysis

The client describes the desired goal and draws a scheme:

`I — goal — problem`

Constellation analysis reveals:

- motivations in the system
- blocks
- cause of difficulties
- possible solution

### Step 2 — Consultation / Remedy Selection

Homeopathy clarifies:

- depth of the problem
- level of the problem: body / emotions / psyche
- how to solve it
- which remedies are effective now

Typical recommendation:
2–3 remedies for one week.

### Step 3 — Situation Correction / Support

Two options:

1. Client buys remedies and takes them.
2. Remote support through remedies placed/activated on a photo.

The manual states that one week is often enough for state change and stabilization.

### Step 4 — Extended Support

Additional alchemical tools may be used:

- crystals
- sephirotic mandalas
- artifacts
- places of power

Purpose:
- strengthen effect
- direct remedy influence more precisely
- reinforce desired qualities
- stabilize effect on the mental level

## 6. Cases Mentioned

### Case 1 — Realization / Investors

Request:
personal manifestation, attention and support from investors.

State changes:
- sheep archetype: cheerful but passive, waiting to be loved and invited
- wolf archetype: confident but watchful
- young bear: calm, confident, slowly moving toward the goal

Remedies:
- Pulsatilla — child waiting for love / inner calm
- Calcarea carbonica — protector / rock of safety / stability and readiness for change
- Gelsemium — anxious observer / guard / reduces fear before important events

### Case 2 — Voice

Request:
work with voice.

Diagnosis:
symptom meaning — fear of rejection; fear of not receiving recognition creates the symptom.

Remedies:
- Psorinum — fear of rejection
- Pulsatilla — desire for love and care
- Aconitum — fear of losing control

### Case 3 — Confidence

Request:
confidence.

State changes:
- wolf behind the fence watching lambs
- lion / king state
- elephant: calm, thoughtful, strong inside, but fear of expression
- tiger: soft, confident, can show aggression if needed

Main theme:
from fear of reality and change toward strength, confidence, and goal movement.

### Case 4 — Loneliness

Request:
powerlessness, not-needed feeling, loneliness.

First remedies:
- Natrum muriaticum — person with broken heart
- Nitricum acidum — offended/wounded one

Later remedy:
- Medorrhinum — rebel, desire for freedom from social norms

Meaning:
shift from loneliness and sadness toward lightness, freedom, self-expression, and energy to fight.
