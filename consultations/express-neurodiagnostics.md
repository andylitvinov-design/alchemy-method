# Express Diagnosis And Neurodiagnostics

This file defines the basic structure of Andrey Li's express diagnosis and neurodiagnostic analysis model.

This is a method and educational framework. It does not replace medical, neurological, psychiatric, or psychological diagnosis or treatment.

---

## 1. Express Diagnosis

Express diagnosis includes analysis of:

1. Which Dao level the person's current psychological structure corresponds to.
2. What the person's nearest developmental tasks are according to the Dao Resource Ladder.
3. The foundation of the psyche: which brainstem / reticular formation centers may need support.
4. How the person can support themselves through body-based exercises.

Core formula:

Dao level → developmental task → neurophysiological foundation → body practice support

---

## 2. Purpose

The goal of express diagnosis is to give the client:

- understanding of their current Dao level
- clarity about nearest developmental tasks
- insight into strong and weak links in the foundation of the psyche
- practical body-based recommendations
- next step in support or personal work

---

## 3. How Client Sends Data

Suggested client instruction:

1. On one sheet of paper, write all floors and core parameters listed below.
2. This can be written as a block diagram or with abbreviations.
3. Circle each parameter.
4. Send a photo of the drawing and a recent photo of yourself.
5. Within several days, the client receives personal analysis.

Analysis output:

- strong and weak links
- Dao level
- what to do next
- body exercises / support suggestions

---

## 4. General Parameters

Before analyzing reticular formation, include general parameters:

- I / Self
- body
- psyche
- health

These show the global system state.

## 7. Link With Dao Resource Ladder

Possible mapping:

- Levels 1–2 often require Floor 1 support: survival, freeze, body state.
- Levels 3–4 often require Floor 2–3 support: holding, tone, structure, defense.
- Levels 5–7 often require Floor 3–4 support: contact, action, axis, attention.
- Levels 8+ may require Floor 5–6 support: clarity, social engagement, leadership, meaning.

This is only a working hypothesis and must be verified personally.

---

## 8. Body Practice Logic

Body support should match the weak link.

Examples:

- deep freeze / DMNV → very soft grounding, warmth, breath without force
- weak body sensing / NTS → body scanning, gentle sensory awareness
- overcontrol / GI → relaxation and safe release
- weak axis / RfP or Vestibular → standing, balance, gentle axis practices
- threat vigilance / PB or PAG → safe boundary visualization, orienting practice
- low activation / ARAS → gentle activation, light movement, rhythm

Do not prescribe intense practices to low-resource clients.

---

## 9. Safety Boundary

This model is a symbolic, energetic, and educational diagnostic framework.

It is not a medical neurological assessment.

If the client has neurological, psychiatric, emergency, or serious medical symptoms, they should seek appropriate professional care.

---

## 10. Main Rule

The neurodiagnostic model is used to identify the foundation of the psyche and choose gentle, level-matched body support.

It must always be integrated with:

- Dao Resource level
- Wu Xing balance
- client state
- safety
- practical next step
