# Bach Essence Profile Template

This file defines the standard format for describing Bach essences in Andrey Li's alchemy method.

Use this template for student materials, client reports, remedy libraries, and symbolic/archetypal descriptions.

Safety:
This is educational and supportive material. It does not replace medical or psychological care and does not make medical guarantees.

---

## Standard Structure

```md
# [ESSENCE NAME]

## Experience / Observed Effect

Describe the lived effect observed in practice.

Example:
The essence gives a deep feeling of comfort. Inner freezing and pain soften. A feeling appears: "I am alive and feeling again."

## Essence

Describe the core energy of the essence.

What does it gather, restore, harmonize, open, soften, or strengthen?

## Meaning

Explain the symbolic and therapeutic meaning.

Use simple, client-friendly language.

## When It May Fit

The essence may fit when a person:

- ...
- ...
- ...

## Developmental Stage

Describe whether it corresponds to:

- any age / timeless support
- early trauma
- shock
- grief
- developmental wound
- specific Dao level or Wu Xing state

## Trauma Core

Write the inner formulas of pain.

Examples:
- "It was too painful."
- "I could not live through it."
- "Something broke inside me."

## Effect

Describe what may soften, return, open, or stabilize.

Use cautious language:
- may support
- may help
- can be used as
- often corresponds to

## Message Of The Essence

Write 3–6 short messages.

## Transformation Image

Describe the symbolic transformation.

## Ritual / Practice

Give a simple ritual or practice.

Use safe wording:
If you use this essence, follow product instructions and personal/professional guidance.

- ...
- ...
- ...
```

## Example — Star Of Bethlehem

# STAR OF BETHLEHEM

## Experience / Observed Effect

Experience shows that this essence can give a deep feeling of comfort.

Inner freezing and pain soften. A feeling appears:

"I am alive and feeling again."

## Essence

Star of Bethlehem softly gathers and harmonizes the inner state.

It carries the energy of comfort, recovery, and healing after shock.

It is the energy of the Star of Bethlehem: a gentle white flower, like a small luminous star.

## Meaning

This is the energy that helps restore wholeness after trauma.

It supports the person in softly living through pain and gathering themselves again.

It corresponds to states where there has been:

- shock
- trauma
- strong emotional impact
- inner freezing
- a painful break in continuity

## When It May Fit

Star of Bethlehem may fit when a person:

- has lived through a strong event
- feels frozen or empty
- cannot let go of the past
- carries pain inside
- feels internally broken
- needs comfort and gentle recovery

## Developmental Stage

Any age. This essence is outside ordinary developmental time.

It can relate to:

- acute trauma
- childhood experiences
- deep shock states
- restoration of psychic wholeness

## Trauma Core

- "It was too painful."
- "I could not live through it."
- "It feels like something broke inside me."

## Effect

The essence may gently support:

- softening consequences of shock
- return of sensitivity
- living through trauma more softly
- restoration of inner wholeness
- a feeling of being whole again

Possible inner feeling:

"I am whole again."

## Message Of The Essence

- You can heal.
- Pain can be lived through.
- You are not broken.
- There is wholeness inside you.
- Light can return gently.

## Transformation Image

Dark night.

Inside there is cold, silence, and emptiness. Everything seems stopped.

Then a soft light appears — a small star.

The light begins to expand. It warms the inner space, and life gradually returns.

## Ritual / Practice

If you use this essence, follow product instructions and personal/professional guidance.

Simple symbolic practice:

1. Place one hand on the heart.
2. Imagine a dark inner space.
3. In the center, a small star appears.
4. With each inhale, it becomes brighter.
5. The light fills the chest, body, and surrounding space.
6. Warmth and softness gradually appear.

## Phrases / Affirmations

- I am healing.
- I accept my pain.
- I become whole again.
- There is light within me.
```

## Style Rules

Good description:

- starts from lived effect
- explains essence simply
- names trauma core
- gives transformation image
- offers safe ritual/practice
- avoids medical guarantees

Avoid:

- presenting the essence as a cure
- rigid prescribing
- unsupported medical claims
- excessive abstraction
- too much theory before the lived state
