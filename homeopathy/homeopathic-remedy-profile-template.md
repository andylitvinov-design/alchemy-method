# Homeopathic Remedy Profile Template

This file defines the standard format for describing homeopathic remedies in Andrey Li's alchemy method.

Use this template for remedy libraries, client reports, student materials, archetypal descriptions, and level-based support maps.

Safety:
This is educational, symbolic, and method-oriented material. It does not replace medical care and does not make medical guarantees. Remedy selection, potency, and use require personal/professional verification.

---

## Example — Saccharum Lactis

# SACCHARUM LACTIS — Milk Sugar

## Observations

In practice, Saccharum lactis has shown itself well in early therapy stages or difficult situations:

- moments of emotional helplessness when the person wants to curl up and avoid speaking with anyone;
- states of bodily illness and weakness when support is needed but difficult to receive;
- inner emptiness and difficulty accepting care.

## Substance / Base

Saccharum lactis is milk sugar, a component of milk.

Symbolically, it relates to:

- basic belonging;
- bodily support;
- the early infant experience of being fed;
- the feeling: "I am needed — I am being nourished."

This is not only nutrition.

It is the first experience of love through the body.

It gives a state of soft fullness:

"I am held — so I can relax — so I am alive."

## Archetype

The Infant / The Accepted One.

This remedy helps touch the state where there is no need to fight.

The world comes toward the person.

## Indications / State

Saccharum lactis may correspond to:

- inner emptiness;
- lack of support;
- dependence on approval;
- exhaustion;
- difficulty receiving help;
- emotional helplessness;
- need to curl up and be held;
- inability to accept nourishment, care, or support.

## Source / Developmental Layer

Developmental layer:
0–1.5 years — contact, feeding, receiving.

Stress formula:

"They will not give me."
"I am not allowed to receive."

## Core Idea

Life can come to you.

You do not have to earn everything.

## Message

- You have the right to receive.
- You can be supported.
- You do not have to survive alone.
- You are allowed to take in nourishment.
- You are needed.

## Images

- Warm milk in a bowl.
- Hands that hold.
- Soft golden light.
- A body wrapped in warmth.
- Quiet breathing nearby.

## Effect / Support Direction

Saccharum lactis may support:

- the feeling "I have enough";
- softening inner emptiness;
- reducing tense dependence;
- returning contact with the body;
- learning to receive without tension;
- basic safety;
- gentle acceptance of care.

## Meditation / Image Practice

You are lying in warmth.

You are being held.

You do not need to do anything, and still everything comes.

## Ritual / Practice

Make a warm drink.

Wrap the body in warmth.

Place one hand on the belly and say:

"I am allowed to receive. I am safe."

## Dao / Wu Xing / Level Connection

Possible connection:

- early Water/Earth support;
- receiving and nourishment;
- lower levels where warmth, holding, and basic support are needed;
- states close to Snow Queen / early Prometheus when the system needs gentle warmth and container.

## Style Rules

Good homeopathic profile:

- starts from practical observations;
- names substance and symbolism;
- identifies archetype;
- identifies developmental layer;
- gives core idea and message;
- uses images and ritual;
- connects to Dao/Wu Xing when relevant;
- avoids rigid prescribing and medical guarantees.

Avoid:

- presenting remedy as a cure;
- unsupported medical claims;
- overconfident potency advice;
- abstract theory without lived state;
- ignoring level and resource capacity.
