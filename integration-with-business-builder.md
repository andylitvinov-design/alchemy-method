# Integration With Business Builder

This file explains how alchemy-method connects with ai-projects-brain.

---

## Two Systems

### 1. alchemy-method

Purpose:
Method, diagnostics, reports, therapy logic.

### 2. ai-projects-brain

Purpose:
Business, offers, landing pages, pricing, marketing.

---

## Rule

Method is always created here.
Business packaging is done in ai-projects-brain.

---

## Workflow

1. Diagnose using alchemy-method
2. Create report / logic
3. Move to ai-projects-brain
4. Build product / offer / landing

---

## Example

alchemy-method:
- Dao level
- Wu Xing
- RF
- remedies

ai-projects-brain:
- offer
- landing
- pricing
- content

---

## Main Principle

Never mix method and marketing in one place.

Keep:
- method clean
- business structured
