# Dao Resource Scale — Daoist Resource Ladder

This file defines the Daoist Resource Ladder used in Andrey Li's alchemy method.

It is a diagnostic and program-building scale for understanding a person's current resource level, next realistic step, and correct intervention.

Core principle:

current level → correct resource → next level → stabilization

---

## 1. Purpose

The Dao Resource Scale helps to:

- diagnose current state
- define resource level
- avoid giving the wrong intervention
- structure consultations and reports
- build monthly programs
- measure growth
- explain progress to clients

The scale is not a rigid label. It is a dynamic map of resource growth.

---

Focus:

Focus:

Resource range:
8.5–10

Levels:
15–18

---

## 3. Resource Ranges

General resource map:

- 1–5 → health resource
- 5–8 → success resource
- 9–10 → spiritual force

Operational interpretation:

- below 3 → unstable survival / collapse / freeze
- 3–5 → stabilization and first movement
- 5–8 → expansion, success, money, relationships
- 8+ → scale, mission, impact
- 9+ → Dao alignment and spiritual presence

---

## 4. Cycle Logic

One Daoist Alchemy cycle usually means:

- movement up by 1 level
- about 1 month
- about 2 sessions

Typical client starting point:

- around level 2

Important:

The goal is not to jump several levels.
The goal is to move one level up and stabilize it.

---

## 5. Stage 1 — Health Alchemy

Stage 1 restores the basic body-health resource and the ability to stay alive, supported, warm, structured, and capable of first contact with the world.

### Level 1 — Storm / Chaos

Resource:
1–2

State:
- illness
- no support
- no strength
- chaos
- energy collapses downward
- the person cannot hold resource

Main task:
Give support.

Work focus:
- basic safety
- grounding
- holding
- crisis stabilization
- reduce collapse

Risk:
Do not demand action or visibility. The system needs support first.

---

### Level 2 — Snow Queen

Resource:
2–2.5

State:
- frozen life
- resource is not active yet
- resource no longer disappears completely
- first point of balance appears

Main task:
Give warmth.

Work focus:
- thawing
- gentle contact
- warmth
- softening freeze
- first life response

Risk:
Do not force strong expression. The system is still frozen.

---

### Level 3 — Prometheus

Resource:
2.5–3.0

State:
- unstable resource
- first filling with strength
- first glow of life
- resource can be felt but is still soft

Main task:
Give structure.

Work focus:
- stabilize first fire
- organize resource
- create rhythm
- prevent resource leak

Risk:
Do not overheat or overload. The flame is new and unstable.

---

### Level 4 — Fortress

Resource:
3.0–3.5

State:
- first axis appears
- person no longer falls so easily into deficit
- first inner support
- protective structure forms

Main task:
Give manifestation.

Work focus:
- first expression
- safe boundaries
- visible presence
- movement from inner support to outer form

Risk:
Do not break the fortress. It is still needed for stability.

---

### Level 5 — King / Queen

Resource:
3.5–4.0

State:
- first impulses outward
- filling space
- self-expression
- free will appears
- identity becomes more visible

Main task:
Give power.

Work focus:
- strengthen will
- support self-expression
- expand personal space
- allow the person to take place

Risk:
Do not shame the emerging self. It is learning to occupy space.

---

### Level 6 — Captain

Resource:
4.0–4.5

State:
- strong enough core
- can declare oneself
- can adjust the world around oneself
- may be slightly rigid or constrained

Main task:
Give softness.

Work focus:
- flexibility
- less control
- more trust
- softer leadership
- ability to navigate without stiffness

Risk:
Do not remove structure too early. Soften it gradually.

---

### Level 7 — Stream

Resource:
4.5–5.0

State:
- interest in contact with the world
- curiosity
- soft interaction
- first movement toward relationship and exchange

Main task:
Give courage.

Work focus:
- safe contact
- curiosity
- relational courage
- movement toward the world

Risk:
Do not push into large success yet. The person is just entering living contact.

2. Current stage.

4. Resource range.
5. Correct intervention.
6. Next level.
7. What must be stabilized.

Stage:

Main state:

Next level:
One-month goal:

---

## 9. How To Use In Reports

Reports should include:

- current Dao Resource level
- explanation in client language
- what this level means
- next level
- what support is needed
- expected movement in one cycle

Example:

Current level: 3 — Prometheus, 2.5–3.0 resource.
The first life force is appearing, but it is still unstable. The main task is not large action yet, but structure: rhythm, support, and stabilization of the first resource.

Next level: 4 — Fortress.
Goal: create a first stable axis so the person does not fall back into deficit.

---

## 10. How To Use In Products

The scale creates a product logic:

1 cycle = 1 level up

Typical product:

- 1 month
- 2 sessions
- diagnosis
- resource correction
- practical steps
- progress tracking

Offer language:

This work helps identify your current resource level and move one realistic step upward: from collapse to support, from freeze to warmth, from instability to structure, from stability to manifestation, or from personal success to deeper mission.

---

## 11. Main Rule

Do not give the wrong resource for the level.

Examples:

- Storm needs support, not ambition.
- Snow Queen needs warmth, not pressure.
- Prometheus needs structure, not expansion.
- Fortress needs safe manifestation, not destruction of defenses.
- Captain needs softness, not more control.
- River needs courage to flow, not forced scale.
- Ocean needs mature scale, not small survival work.

Correct work means:

identify level → give matching resource → move one level up → stabilize.
