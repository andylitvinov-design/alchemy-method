# Master Method — Andrey Li Alchemy Method

This document is the source of truth for Andrey Li's method library.

It describes the core logic behind consultations, homeopathic/archetypal analysis, Wu Xing resource diagnostics, psychodynamic interpretation, alchemical transformation, reports, programs, and practice materials.

This is a method document, not a sales page.

---

## 1. Core Purpose

The method helps identify the hidden mechanism behind a person's difficulty, restore the missing resource, and support movement to the next level of life, health, money, relationships, business, or self-realization.

Core formula:

client state → hidden mechanism → resource → next level → practical action/result

Short version:

Find the internal failure → restore the system → increase resource → move to a higher level.

---

## 2. Main View Of A Human System

A person is seen as a living system where psyche, body, energy, behavior, relationships, money, and meaning are connected.

A symptom, block, repeated life situation, or business stagnation is not treated only as an isolated problem. It is read as a signal of a system mechanism.

The method asks:

- What impulse wants to move?
- Where does the system compress?
- What defense appears?
- What repeats?
- Where does resource leak or freeze?
- What quality must be restored?
- What next level is realistic?

---

## 3. Problem Cycle

The basic problem cycle:

Impulse → Compression → Defense → Repetition → Exhaustion

### Impulse

The original movement of life:
- desire
- need
- growth impulse
- love
- action
- visibility
- money movement
- sexuality
- creativity
- will
- contact
- self-realization

### Compression

The point where the system closes, freezes, contracts, or becomes blocked.

Compression can appear as:
- fear
- shame
- guilt
- tension
- numbness
- avoidance
- body symptom
- loss of energy
- confusion
- inability to act

### Defense

The protection strategy that forms around compression.

Examples:
- withdrawal
- control
- perfectionism
- pleasing
- collapse
- intellectualization
- avoidance
- over-effort
- emotional shutdown
- waiting for permission

### Repetition

The old pattern keeps recreating itself in life.

It may repeat in:
- health
- money
- business
- relationships
- creativity
- visibility
- self-worth
- decision-making

### Exhaustion

The visible result:
- low energy
- no movement
- illness pressure
- burnout
- stagnation
- repeated failure
- loss of clarity
- loss of confidence

## 4. Growth Cycle

The basic growth cycle:

Awareness → Release → Resource → New Behavior → Stabilization → Expansion

### Awareness

The hidden mechanism becomes visible.

The person understands:
- what is happening
- where the system compresses
- what defense repeats
- what resource is missing

### Release

The system begins to reduce compression and automatic reaction.

Release can happen through:
- psychotherapeutic insight
- emotional processing
- body awareness
- symbolic work
- remedy support
- ritual/symbolic act
- practical behavioral step

### Resource

The missing quality is restored or strengthened.

Resource may be:
- safety
- energy
- action
- joy
- support
- clarity
- boundaries
- value
- will
- confidence

### New Behavior

Internal change must translate into real action.

Examples:
- clear decision
- message to client
- price increase
- boundary
- rest
- visibility step
- creative expression
- health-supporting routine

### Stabilization

The new state becomes more reliable.

The person stops returning immediately to old compression and can hold a stronger level.

### Expansion

The person moves to the next level:
- more energy
- more clarity
- more money movement
- more confidence
- more contact
- more creative and spiritual expression

## 5. Wu Xing Resource Model

Wu Xing is used as a practical diagnostic map of resource.

It is not used as abstract philosophy. It helps identify where the system is weak, excessive, blocked, frozen, or ready to grow.

### Water — Base / Energy / Safety / Deep Reserve

Water shows:
- vitality
- deep energy
- safety
- trust in life
- will to live
- rest and recovery
- deep reserve

When Water is weak:
- exhaustion
- fear
- collapse
- coldness
- no reserve
- lack of basic support

### Wood — Growth / Direction / Action

Wood shows:
- initiative
- direction
- movement
- growth
- will
- ability to start and continue action

When Wood is blocked:
- irritation
- stuck action
- no direction
- procrastination
- internal pressure
- inability to move forward

### Fire — Expression / Visibility / Joy / Contact

Fire shows:
- joy
- visibility
- inspiration
- connection
- social warmth
- expression
- charisma

When Fire is blocked:
- fear of being seen
- shame
- loss of joy
- isolation
- social anxiety
- lack of inspiration

### Earth — Stability / Support / Grounding / Material Base

Earth shows:
- stability
- nourishment
- digestion of experience
- routine
- home
- material support
- ability to receive and hold

When Earth is weak:
- instability
- anxiety
- lack of grounding
- poor routine
- difficulty receiving support
- scattered life structure

### Metal — Clarity / Value / Boundaries / Structure

Metal shows:
- clarity
- boundaries
- precision
- value
- structure
- self-respect
- ability to separate and choose

When Metal is weak:
- confusion
- weak boundaries
- low value
- difficulty saying no
- unclear offer
- lack of structure

## 6. Resource Scale

The method measures movement by levels.

Basic scale:

- 2.0 — compression / fear / shutdown
- 3.0 — first stabilization / first support
- 4.0 — confidence / self-management / stronger support
- 5.0 — movement / growth / action
- 6.0 — flow / stable growth / stronger state

The goal is not to jump to an unrealistic ideal.

The goal is:

current level → next realistic level

Example:

2.8 — compression, confusion, low action
→ 4.0 — support, clarity, confidence, first stable movement

## 9. Alchemical Layer

Alchemy is used as a language of transformation.

It helps describe how a state changes:

- from raw material to refined form
- from chaos to structure
- from compression to resource
- from shadow to quality
- from symptom to meaning
- from blocked impulse to conscious action

Core alchemical movements:

- separation: distinguish symptom, defense, impulse, resource
- purification: reduce distortion, fear, shame, excess reaction
- integration: return rejected parts into the system
- fixation: stabilize the new state
- transmutation: turn pain, block, or conflict into resource and direction

Alchemy should be practical, not decorative.

Use it to clarify transformation, not to obscure the work.

## 16. Safety And Boundaries

This method does not replace medical care, psychotherapy, psychiatry, emergency care, financial advice, or legal advice.

Do not claim:

- guaranteed healing
- guaranteed income
- guaranteed cure
- instant transformation
- one remedy solves everything

Use careful language:

- may support
- can help understand
- can help identify
- supports resource and clarity
- needs verification
- seek appropriate professional care when needed

Do not store private client data in public repositories.
